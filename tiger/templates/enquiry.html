We know that pursuing your passions or dream career on your own can become lonely and discouraging. The struggles, triumphs and challenges, can sometimes feel so real and overwhelming that you might want to share it with people who understand or have been there before in order to feel a sense of support and belonging, to be a part of a tribe that cares.

This is who we are.

At Flinter, we not only want to create a safe space for all kinds of hustlers (solopreneurs, entrepreneurs, passion project starters, career go-getters), but a strong space to imbue courage as well as comfort for you hustlers when the going gets tough.

At Flinter, you can look forward to:

Showcasing your expertise

Take to Flinter to share your burning thoughts or showcase what you’ve been doing, what you’re good at, or even out-of-the-blue topics that you might want some feedback from. It’s about time you get that recognition!

Staying on top of new opportunities

When you’re on Flinter and have established good content by displaying your potential, you’ll get opportunities to collaborate. Start a new baby project, brainstorm, have discussions. This is all so exciting!

Being supported by our Community

It can be tough to start out on your own. In Flinter, our community provides like-minded support, people who have been through what you have, so you never have to face challenges alone.

We provide more emphasis on growth and learning, not simply focusing on performance. Our collaborative community is here to give you the resources you need to build your business or career in the direction that you are passionate about. We are here to be a source of questions when defining the edge of your capabilities, as well as advice when seeking a solid understanding of your identity. We celebrate when two or more Flinters birth new collaborative projects or many have found new income opportunities because of this Community.

We aspire to help career hustlers to enjoy their pursuit of what drives them and be the springboard for new friendships, new collaborations and new joys in being a Career Hustler.
